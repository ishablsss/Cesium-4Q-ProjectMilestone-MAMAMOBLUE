● You must make a password based on the rules given by the game.

● If you want to play again under the same username, you can't use the same password.

WHAT IS THIS GAME?!

THE PASSWORD GAME turns a simple password into total chaos. It starts easy… then suddenly throws crazy rules at you... numbers, symbols, uppercase letters, and more. Just when you think you're done, another rule appears and ruins everything! It’s frustrating, funny, and super addictive. Can you survive the madness and create the ultimate password?
